The Venue

The Ceremony and Reception will take place at:

The Barn at Harburn
Harburn Estate
West Calder
West Lothian
EH55 8RN

The venue can be accessed at the crossroad just north of Harburn village. Pass the village hall and follow the signs for the Barn. Sat Nav works well - just search for “Harburn Barn” in Google Maps.

View on Google Maps

View on Google Maps

There is a car parking area next to the courtyard, with additional space at the side of the road.

For those not driving to the venue, the nearest train station is West Calder, which is on the Glasgow Central - Edinburgh line.

There are also a number of local taxi companies to use:

Fair Deal Cabs 01506 499 111
All The Fours 01506 444 444
